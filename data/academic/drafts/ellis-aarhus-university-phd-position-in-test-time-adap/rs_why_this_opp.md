## Why Aarhus University

Aarhus University stands out as a leading institution in advancing AI research, particularly in the domain of Test-Time Adaptation which is pivotal to the future of resilient AI systems. My professional background, particularly my experience in developing behaviourally-informed agent-based models, aligns closely with the university's emphasis on agentic AI opportunities, focusing on adaptive response mechanisms in complex environments.

The outlined project seeks to understand and enhance agentic behaviours in AI, an area where my expertise in data science applications, particularly in operational environments like licensed banking and governance, can contribute significantly. My recent dissertation on enhancing supply chain systems using transformers and smart contracts has equipped me with unique insights into integrating advanced AI methodologies and longitudinal behaviour modelling.

Moreover, my work has demonstrated the application of operational research techniques in real-world settings, which complements Aarhus’s focus on interdisciplinary strategies in AI research. This synergy positions me to effectively contribute to ongoing projects at Aarhus University while also exploring innovative applications of test-time adaptation in agentic AI.
