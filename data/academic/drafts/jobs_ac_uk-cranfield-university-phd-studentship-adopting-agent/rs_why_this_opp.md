## Why Cranfield University

The PhD Studentship at Cranfield University, focusing on adopting Agentic AI for building supply chain resilience, aligns seamlessly with my academic and professional journey. My MSc dissertation, supervised by Dr Ammar Al-Bazi at Aston University, explored innovative approaches to enhance supply chain information systems through the integration of blockchain and LLMs. This experience directly informs the fundamental aspects of the proposed research, particularly in developing algorithms that enhance resilience through intelligent, behaviourally-responsive decision-making.

My previous work with the Crowd-Shipping Agent-Based Model (https://github.com/nauvalZulfikar/Crowd-Shipping-ABM-) demonstrates my capability in designing simulations that mimic complex logistic systems. I aim to leverage this expertise to implement Agentic AI solutions that adapt to dynamic conditions and stakeholder behaviours within supply chains, thereby addressing the overarching challenge of resilience in logistics. 

Cranfield University's focus on applied research in supply chain management uniquely positions it as the ideal environment for my PhD journey. The strategic integration of AI and supply chain resilience is a burgeoning field, and I am eager to contribute to its advancement.
