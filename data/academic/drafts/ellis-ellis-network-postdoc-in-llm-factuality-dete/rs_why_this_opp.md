## Why ELLIS Network

The ELLIS Network's focus on LLM factuality detection aligns with my strong background in natural language processing and machine learning. The project's emphasis on improving LLM output accuracy resonates with my MSc dissertation, where I employed DeBERTa-v3 for sentiment analysis in supply chain contexts, demonstrating my capability to implement advanced NLP techniques. Integrating factuality detection into LLMs presents a crucial frontier for responsible AI, an area I am passionate about and have explored through my recent projects.

Utilising techniques such as sentiment analysis and decision-rule generation via Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), as exemplified in my GitHub repo 'LLM-Shipper-Profiles', I am adept at applying these methods to practical problems. The collaboration with the University of Copenhagen would provide an excellent platform to further advance my research in the detection of factual inconsistencies, thereby contributing to the field of trustworthy AI. I am eager to contribute my experience in model governance and validation to support the ELLIS mission.
