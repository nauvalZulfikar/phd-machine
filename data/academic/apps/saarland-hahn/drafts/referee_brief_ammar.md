### What this PhD is About
This PhD programme at Saarland University focuses on advancing the theory and interpretability of large language models (LLMs). Under the guidance of Dr. Michael Hahn, the research will delve into mechanistically reverse-engineering model internals and understanding the learning biases of language models, with a bridge to computational cognition.
