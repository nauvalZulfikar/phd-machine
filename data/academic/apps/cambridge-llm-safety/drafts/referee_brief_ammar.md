### What this PhD is about

This PhD studentship focuses on enhancing the safety of Large Language Models (LLMs) by exploring themes such as chain-of-thought faithfulness, detecting deceptive model behaviour, and inference-time safety monitoring. The project is part of the Cambridge LLM Safety group, which aims to ensure transparency and mitigate risks associated with LLMs.
