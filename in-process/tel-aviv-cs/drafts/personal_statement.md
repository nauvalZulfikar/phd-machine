## Technical and Research Experience

During my MSc, I conducted a dissertation titled "Enhancing Supply Chain Information Sharing Systems Through Blockchain: Integrated Customer Reviews LLM Analysis: A Smart Contract Approach." This project involved fine-tuning the DeBERTa-v3 transformer on multi-tier supplier review data and developing an Ethereum smart-contract architecture to improve transparency in supply chains. This work not only deepened my understanding of NLP and blockchain technologies but also highlighted the potential of LLMs in transforming supply chain management.

In addition to my dissertation, I have developed a portfolio of applied LLM projects. Notably, I created the LLM-Generated Adaptive Shipper Decision Rules system, which leverages OpenAI's gpt-4o-mini and retrieval-augmented generation techniques to produce literature-grounded decision rules for shippers. This project, along with others like the CV-JD Suitability Checker and the Crowd-Shipping ABM, showcases my ability to apply advanced machine learning techniques to real-world problems.
